Written description:

I have suffered from cystitis in a few episodes in my life. Specifically I was at home in Italy and I happened to be in pain. It was not a sharp pain but I had a strong feeling of discomfort. I contacted the doctor and he suggested I take the antibiotic (Monuril) before bedtime and blueberry supplements for an extended period of time. I noticed that diet impacts my physical condition a lot. For example, intake of too much coffee (e.g., American Coffee) and fried foods do not help. I also do not usually hydrate and I know that this is something I should pay attention to.

Oral description:

“Mine is is linked to a epileptic seizure I had, so I don't know if you know all the symptoms, but I started with the pyramids because it's pointy, I need something pointy for the headache. Yeah, basically. And then I used the wood ball in the middle because of the heaviness of the pain. And I used the cube because of the nausea. And I see it as something very huge. I wanted something that could flow, but it shapes [does not] work, so I took something heavy that's on top. And I wanted a flat base and I forgot to note it, but because it would be better if it was a cube but not a cube but a parallelogram, so flatter.”
