Written description:

Almost 8 years ago I experienced UTI. I felt a lot of pain in my bladder and kidney. I could not walk up right. I had to bend my torso and be hunched in order to move around because my bladder and kidney was hurting. I didn’t take any medication and I did not go to the doctor and it went away in a few days on its own. I remember experiencing this pain for the first time, and asking my older sister and her friends who happened to be around at the time at my place. I shared my symptoms and they told me it was UTI. I wasn’t sure if they knew what I was from personal experience or if it was because they were majoring in anatomy and biology in university. After experiencing UTI, I was a bit more aware of what could potentially cause this and cared more for my hygiene and my partner’s hygiene during sex. I also now have the habit of peeing right after sex.

Oral description:

“For me, I experienced… it’s sort of like pain when peeing. So for me these objects sort of like represented like spikiness, irregular shapes and all these things are associated to like the pain that I felt during peeing. And then I just wrote, I would want to colour them. [...] because these neutral colour doesn't describe like the pain for me. And so that was the first item that I experienced and then I experienced like the swollen kidney and bladder. And for me, I just sort of representative with like smaller cubes that are like more rubbery and stretchy and the bigger cube that's connected with the very short clear tube because and I was like, hunched over like not being able to be, like, flexible and expand and myself. So I just felt very constricted. And here I just said I wanted to change this to more like organic shape and rather than something like rigid with sharp edges. So…”
