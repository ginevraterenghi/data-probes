Written description:

I would like to share one of my experiences. I was only 22 years old. The first time I had sex with my boyfriend it was also his first sexual relationship. I don't know how sex education works in other countries. But in ** in the past, we didn't have much knowledge about it, we didn't talk about it in school, and our parents didn't tell us anything. Before we had sex, we only knew about buying condoms and showering. But when we opened the condom, we all felt that it smelled bad. So we didn't use condoms. I remember being so shy, I was too embarrassed to even go to the toilet to pee. After the sex, I remember holding my pee all night. After a day, I felt a burning sensation when I urinated, I thought I had a cold, I drank a lot of water, but it didn't help. The next day I saw blood in my urine and by then I was already feeling more pain. I didn't know what was wrong with me and I was too embarrassed to ask my mother, so I asked a sister who was seven years older than me. But she had no such experience as well, so we went to the pharmacy to buy medicine together. I've forgotten which medicines. I remember I bought three. I was afraid to go to the hospital, I wanted to buy several ones to try which one would be useful. Fortunately, after 3-4 days of taking the medication, my symptoms subsided. Then the symptoms slowly disappeared. I hope it won't happen again. Now I take good care of my body and keep it clean and urinate on time.
